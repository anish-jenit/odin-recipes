Briyani Recipe

Ingredients

  • Cloves
  • Cardamom
  • Cinnamon
  • Small Onion
  • Coconut Oil
  • Mutton
  • Tomato
  • Green Chilli
  • Mustard Seeds
  • Coriander leaves
  • Ginger garlic paste
  • Mint leaves
  • Curd
  • Chilli, Turmeric, Garam powder
  • salt
  1. Grind cloves, cardamom and cinnamon in a mixer
  2. Grind small onions
  3. Heat oil in a cooker and add in all the spices along with bay leaf
  4. Add onion paste and sliced onions along with ginger garlic paste and salt
  5. After onions turn brown, add the sliced tomatoes
  6. After tomatoes turn mushy, add in chilly, turmeric and garam masala powder
  7. Add mutton pieces to cooker and simmer for 5 mins
  8. Add green chillies, curd and water
  9. Mix well, close the pressure cooker and wait for 8 whistles
  10. Once the pressure is off, open the cooker lid and add chopped coriander and mint leaves
  11. Add in rice and cook for 15 mins in medium flame without the lid
  12. Once the rice is half cooked, squeeze lemon juice inside
  13. Simmer for 15-20 mins with lid closed
  14. Serve the hot Mutton Briyani and show off
