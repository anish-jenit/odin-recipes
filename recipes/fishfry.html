Fish Fry Recipe

Ingredients

  • lemon
  • Oil
  • Curry leaves
  • Turmeriuc, Chilli, Pepper powder
  • salt
  • fish

Procedure

  1. Marinate fish with lemon, curry leaves, turmeric, chilly and pepper powder
  2. Heat oil in a pan and add oil as required
  3. Fry the fish in medium flame
  4. Serve in style and show off
