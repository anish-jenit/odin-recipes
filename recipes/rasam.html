Rasam Recipe

Ingredients

  • Garlic
  • Cumin
  • Tomato
  • Green Chilly
  • Oil
  • Mustard seeds
  • Tamarind paste
  • Curry leaves
  • Coriander leaves
  • Turmeriuc, Chilli, Pepper
  • Asofetida
  • salt

Procedure

  1. Grind tomatoes, Cumin and Garlic in a mixer
  2. Heat oil in a pan and add Cumim Mustard Seeds, Asofetida, curry leaves
  3. Add the grinded paste from step 1
  4. Add turmeric, clilly and pepper powder along with salt
  5. Add some water and turmeric paste
  6. Add crushed garlic and let the mixture cook in low flame
  7. Once boiled add corriander leaves and turn off the stove
  8. Serve in style and show off
